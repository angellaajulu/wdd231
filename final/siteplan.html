Kampala Local Tourism Guide – Site Plan

Site plan Document for the Web Project

Site Name

Kampala Local Tourism Guide

This site name was selected because it clearly represents a guide focused on tourism within Kampala, Uganda.

Optional domain: kampalalocaltourism.com

Site Purpose

Provide visitors with clear and reliable information about tourism in Kampala. The site highlights popular attractions, cultural sites, parks, entertainment areas, and provides basic travel tips and contact information.

Scenarios

  • What popular tourist attractions can I visit while staying in Kampala?
  • Where can I find cultural sites, parks, and entertainment places in the city?

Color Scheme

  • Green (#2e7d32): Headings, navigation, and footer
  • Light Gray (#f2f6fa): Backgrounds
  • Warm Gold (#e6a23c): Highlights and call-to-action areas

Typography

  • Playfair Display: Headings
  • Roboto: Body text and paragraphs
